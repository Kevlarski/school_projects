Slot Jaw
A shop standard, these tongs are just flat jaws with a slot in the middle.
  • Decent for grabbing most things
  • Best for flat material
  • Ususally the first pair one makes
Bolt Jaw
Tongs with a wide gap in the jaws to make room for bolt-heads.
  • Designed to grab material with a long shank
  • Easy to grab rod and smaller bar stock
  • Great utility tongs
Square Jaw
Another great set of utility tongs.
  • Decent for grabbing most stock
  • Made with a specific size when assembled
  • Best for the specific size the jaws are made for
Scroll
Narrow jawed tongs for precision work.
  • Small jaws to minimize contact with material
  • Used for pinpoint accuracy when bending or scrolling
  • A specialized set, but also decent utility set
Rail Spike
A very specialized set of tongs.
  • Specifically designed to hold railroad spikes
  • Very poor for most other material
  • The best option to use for an often unweildy material
